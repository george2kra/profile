Warehoue inventory

This simple warehouse inventory program was written in Ruby, a line command application that allows the user to locate a free shelf for pallet placement of bulk goods according to height and weight of the pallet.

The details are recorded and placed into an array for later retrieval.

The customer is able to list (locate goods), move, or shipout pallets as required.

The customer is able to customize their rack locations with specific unique indentification numbers, maximun shelve heights, and their weight limitations.

They are also able to freeze racks or shelves from use, remove shelving from database, or even add new shelving when expanding their warehouse storage.

Further detials can be added such as secure locations, refrigerated shelves, or shelves for storing dangerous goods.
